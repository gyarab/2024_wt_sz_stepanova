Carbonara

What is it?

Carbonara is a pasta dish made with fatty cured pork, hard cheese, eggs, salt, and black pepper. It is typical of the Lazio region of Italy.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • guanciale (or pancetta)
  • hard cheese (usually pecorino romano, occasionally Parmesan or Grana Padano, or a mixture)
  • eggs
  • black pepper

Method

Cook spaghetti until al dente. In a bowl, whisk eggs, Parmesan, and black pepper. Saut√© pancetta until crispy.

Toss hot pasta with pancetta, then quickly mix in the egg mixture to create a creamy sauce. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper. Enjoy!
