Bolognese

What is it?

Bolognese sauce, known in Italian as ragù alla bolognese or ragù bolognese, is a meat-based sauce in Italian cuisine, typical of the city of Bologna.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • ground meat
  • celery
  • carrot
  • onion
  • tomato paste
  • red wine
  • milk

Method

Sauté onions, garlic, and carrots in olive oil. Add ground beef, cook until browned, then stir in tomatoes, wine, and herbs. Simmer for 1-2 hours.

Cook pasta until al dente, then toss with the sauce. Serve with grated Parmesan and fresh basil. Enjoy your hearty Bolognese!
